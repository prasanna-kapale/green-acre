Check-Out Policy

  • Guests must vacate the premises immediately at the conclusion of their 12-hour block (8:00 AM or 8:00 PM sharp).
  • Late check-out requests must be made 24 hours in advance and are subject to availability and additional charges.
  • The maintenance team requires time to prepare the property for the next group.

Cleanliness & Property Care

  • Trash must be discarded in designated disposal bins before departure.
  • Heavy stains on linens, upholstery, or surfaces will incur cleaning deduction charges.
  • Any property damage will be assessed and billed at replacement cost.
  • Outdoor areas including the pool deck, ground, and parking must be left tidy.

Pool & Amenities Safety

  • Appropriate swimming attire is required at all times in the pool.
  • Children must be supervised by adults at all times in the pool and kids' play zone.
  • No glass items are permitted within the pool area.
  • Swimming after consuming alcohol is strongly discouraged.

General House Rules

  • Music and outdoor activity must end by 11:00 PM out of respect for the surrounding area.
  • Guests are responsible for the behaviour of all individuals in their group.
  • The property is strictly no-smoking indoors. Smoking is permitted only in designated outdoor areas.
  • Pets are not permitted on the property.
